Enter Multiple Orders

The Enter Multiple Orders Window

Multiple orders may be entered at one time using this window. Orders may be typed in manually, or pasted in from another source. When Accept is clicked, all orders are processed and the orders that failed and the reason it failed are displayed.

Hints and Caveats

Orders for different powers may be entered on the same screen, except in Face-to-Face games. Only one order is allowed per line. jDip makes every effort to parse a recognizable order into a valid order; non alphanumeric characters and odd spacing is ignored. Extraneous characters at the beginning of an order are also usually ignored.
